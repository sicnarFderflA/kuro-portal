Research Grants Submission Process

1
The proposal must follow the provided proposal template (XURG Form 1), otherwise the proposal may be rejected without technical review.
2
The proposal must be submitted together with the following: endorsement form (XURG Form 2), one-page curriculum vitae of the principal investigator/project leader and all research team members, and if applicable survey questionnaire/data matrix.
3
An incomplete application may be rejected. Please contact KURO through email at kinaadman@xu.edu.ph or at local extension 9167 for questions or assistance.
4
MR proposals are required to have a lead component (program) proposal, and a separate proposal for each component project.
📌 Note: A program is a group of inter-related R&D projects requiring an interdisciplinary, multidisciplinary, or transdisciplinary approach to meet established goal(s) within a specific time frame. A project, on the other hand, is a basic unit in the investigation of a specific R&D problem with predetermined objectives to be accomplished within a specific time frame.
